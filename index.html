Introduction

What is IRPG?

The IRPG app is used to submit weather reports to improve safety and efficiency fireline. and For the first time ever, the IRPG is searchable! Find what you are looking for in record time by utilizing the search bar at the top of that app.

In - App Features

  • Mobile Optimized

    Designed from the ground up to support all of the latest iOS and Android smartphones

  • Powerful Search

    Full-text search of all IRPG pages through an easy-to-use search bar at the top of the app

  • In-App Notes

    Take notes inside your IRPG without needing to carry a pen! Notes are saved in the app

  • Bookmarks

    One-tap bookmarks to keep the clutter down and allow for quick reference of key pages.

  • Weather Reporting

    Use your phone's GPS to send precise weather updates via SMS or iMessage to your STL.

  • Checklists

    Selectable checklists for common and crucial radio traffic, such as sizeups.

Submit Weather Reports

Use the IRPG App to submit your fireline weather reports over SMS, freeing up valuable radio traffic
